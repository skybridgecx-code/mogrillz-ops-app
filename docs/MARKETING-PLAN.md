# Shama's Kitchen Meal Prep — 90-Day Marketing Plan

Built from what actually grows meal prep companies: partnerships/referrals (up to ~45% of new customers for the best operators, with ~37% better retention than ad-acquired customers), community presence, weekly-cadence content, and email lifecycle. Paid ads come last, after organic proves the offer.

---

## Phase 0 — Get ready (Weeks 1–2)

- **Photos first.** One shoot: 12 meals plated + labeled containers + kitchen BTS. Food photos are the entire game on Instagram.
- **Set up the offer:** Founding Member deal — *20% off your first 4 weeks + locked founding price*. Scarcity: first 50 members.
- **Landing page** on mogrillzva.vercel.app: weekly menu, macros, halal sourcing statement, order deadline, founding offer, email capture.
- **Activate the list you already own:** your email-updates subscribers get the founding offer 48 hours before anyone else. (The copy-emails + draft button is already in the ops app → Customers.)
- **Claim profiles:** Google Business Profile ("halal meal prep Herndon/NoVA"), Yelp, Nextdoor business page.

## Phase 1 — Launch (Weeks 3–6)

**Community (highest ROI, zero spend):**
- Flyers + sample drops: Spice Village food hall area, halal supermarkets (Halal Bazar Fairfax, HalalWay, Ghazaly Manassas), masjid community boards (ADAMS Center, Dar Al-Hijrah areas).
- Post weekly menu in DMV desi/Muslim Facebook groups + Nextdoor every Monday; order-deadline reminder Thursday morning.
- Ask the first 20 customers for a Google review + tagged story; repost everything.

**Referral program from day one:** Give $15 / Get $15 off a 5-pack. Community-dense audiences refer at exceptional rates — make it stupid-easy (a code on every box label).

**Content cadence (IG + TikTok, 4 posts/week):**
1. *Sunday:* weekly menu drop reel (this is your anchor — same time every week)
2. *Tuesday:* macro card or meal close-up ("42g protein chicken karahi bowl")
3. *Thursday:* "order deadline tonight" story + BTS prep clip
4. *Saturday:* cook-day kitchen video — fire, smoke, volume ("cooked Saturday, in your fridge Sunday")

**Angles that hit both audiences:**
- "Halal meal prep that doesn't taste like punishment"
- "Your macros, your mom's flavors"
- "Stop guessing if it's actually zabihah"
- Family tray angle: "Weeknight dinner for 4, solved — $11 a plate"

## Phase 2 — Partnerships & retention (Weeks 7–12)

**Gym/trainer channel (the Territory Foods playbook):**
- Target: gyms near halal-dense zips (Herndon, Chantilly, Fairfax, Sterling, Ashburn), MMA/boxing gyms, CrossFit boxes, personal trainers with Muslim clientele.
- Offer: free sample day + trainer referral deal (15% of first order, 5% ongoing). Goal: 3 signed gym partners by day 90. One exclusive "official meal prep partner" deal beats five loose ones.

**Office lunch accounts:** one 10-person office = $600–1,000/week recurring. Pitch tech offices in Reston/Herndon corridor: weekly team lunch drop, one invoice.

**Email lifecycle (churn killer):**
- Welcome → what to expect + how to reheat
- Week 2: check-in + "rate your meals" (feeds menu rotation)
- Week 4: milestone + referral nudge
- Lapsed 3 weeks: win-back with new-menu highlights (menu fatigue is the #1 quit reason — show them what's *new*)

**Seasonal moats no competitor can touch:** Ramadan suhoor/iftar packs, Eid family trays, cricket World Cup watch-party trays. Plan these into the calendar now — they're your biggest spike weeks.

## Budget guide (first 90 days)

| Item | Est. |
|---|---|
| Photo/video shoot (one pro half-day, then phone content) | $400–800 |
| Sampling (food cost for ~200 samples) | $500 |
| Flyers/labels/box stickers | $200 |
| Meta/local ads (only after week 8, retarget site visitors + lookalikes) | $500–1,000/mo |
| Referral credits | scales with growth (good problem) |

## Weekly scoreboard (5 numbers, every Monday)

1. Orders this week / vs last week
2. New customers vs returning (target: 50%+ returning by week 8)
3. Referral-attributed orders
4. Email list size
5. IG follower + save count on menu-drop post
